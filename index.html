Nujabes

"DJ Seba Jun and his Spirirtual Journey through Hip-Hop"

Seba Jun was one of the most prolific contributors to the background music and soundtracks of the critically acclaimed anime series Samurai Champloo, which blends a feudal Japan setting with modern anachronisms, especially in regard to hip hop culture such as graffiti and rapping.
